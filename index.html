About Us

Based in Chennai, we are a family-run tannery with over 24 years of expertise in crafting exceptional Goat, Sheep, and Cow leather in both Crust and Finished forms. Renowned for our commitment to quality and sustainability, we proudly export to several countries, with a strong focus on China, Germany, and Italy. Our GOLD rating from the Leather Working Group (LWG) underscores our dedication to environmentally responsible practices and excellence in leather production. We collaborate with leading global brands to supply premium-grade leather for Shoes (Upper and Lining), Apparels, and Gloves. Our products are celebrated for their superior quality, competitive pricing, and compliance with REACH and EU standards, ensuring they meet the rigorous demands of international markets. Combining tradition with innovation, we take pride in delivering leather that embodies durability, elegance, and precision, making us a trusted partner in the global leather industry.
